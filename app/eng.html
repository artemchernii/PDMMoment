On March 14 and 15, another edition of the PDM Group Retreat was held!

Interview to HUGO MARÇAL | Sendit General Manager
Sendit
Hugo has been with the company since 2007 and is formed in Computer and Informatics Engineering. He states that what motivates him daily at a professional level is to have the opportunity of doing the work he likes, embrace challenges out of his comfort zone, work with an excellent team and contribute decisively to the success of Sendit.
When was Sendit founded, how have been the company’s journey and how is its adaptation process going within the Go4Mobility and the PDM Group?
“Sendit was founded in 2004. Concerning its journey, we can talk about the before and after Go4 world, but I think we should focus on the world after Go4. The years of 2017 and 2018 were, absolutely, of adaptation. In each one's mind, there were competing companies and platforms that have now joined forces to strengthen their market position. The entry into the group resulted in a natural and positive adjustment, in order to align with the strategic ideas and objectives of the direction. In practice, the work of fusion continued in 2018 and consisted, objectively, in the consolidation of companies’ strategies, standardization of working methods, optimization of processes and definition of a solid path for the Go4 subgroup.

In fact, it was a year where we were consolidating worlds and cultures and, nowadays, we already feel part of the group and like we are one.”
What were the most significant changes in the company after joining the PDM Group? What do you consider to be the advantages of belonging to the Group?
“Belonging to the PDM Group gives us, among other things, a significant competitive advantage by the possibility of having the technic resources nearby. That is, we do not need to spend a lot of time on technical recruitment, which in our business is an important factor. I highlight the excellent collaboration environment that we have found from the first minute we joined the Group in 2017 and, since then, we feel supported at all levels.

Being present in a Group with the experience, dimension, maturity and quality of PDM is very important to learn, improve and achieve the objectives in a faster and more effective way.”
How do you describe your team and which skills do you want to develop better in 2019?
"I believe that it may exist a perception that there is a Sendit team and another Go4, but in reality they are practically one. We share services and departments, such as Marketing and Finance, we just do not share the commercial and technical teams. The first one due to strategic orientation and the second one because we work on different technologies. We have an excellent environment and I can emphasize the mutual help and team spirit. All of us, in general, get along very well with each other. We accept ourselves even when there are divergences of opinion or method. We end up talking and, through constructive criticism, we “guide our boat to good port.

I consider that we have to improve the internal communication. That is to say, we talk to each other and we are very much in tune, but many times, it happens only between closed groups (heads, operatives, technical teams) and if the decisions are not communicated in a correct and linear way, it leads to misunderstandings. This is a clear example of a positive situation that becomes negative because of the misperception of communication, by those who did not participate actively in the decision process. Consequently, it is a challenge that we have been working on. When we make decisions, they should be reported to the teams in a coherent, clear and objective way, so that there are no doubts or questions remaining.”

“Today, we already feel part of the Group and like we are one.”

Do you have in mind any significant change or relevant investment planned for 2019?
“Internally, I highlight the creation of a Human Resources department as an important investment in the company, along with Go4Mobility, besides the restructuring and uniformisation of many processes, whose purpose is to simplify and optimize our day by day. We believe in a sustainable growth and, when with think about expansion, we want to be sure that we are capable of respond to every level and not only at a technical level. In the particular case of Human Resources, we believe in the immense value of our team and that is why we want to join forces to provide even better working conditions.

At an external level, we want to continue the internationalization process in Mozambique and Spain, where we launched, during 2018, our uSendit platform and where we have been investing in the establishment of local teams, focused in the brand visibility and commercialization of our products. These markets are strategic for us, but the international goal is more ambitious and we pretend for the platform to be commercialized online in any part of the world, covering different needs at a language, currency and payment methods level. In any case, besides the products that we already have, we look closely to the diverse innovations in the sector (Marketing Automation, RCS, WhatsApp4Business), opportunities and needs, looking for developing solutions that can complement our offer and that can distinguish our company as an organization that can create real value to the clients. We have as goal to promote Sendit as a company that is not just a SMS or Email services supplier, but a reference company for Communication and Digital Marketing solutions, properly specialized and capable of responding to the demands of the various activity sectors.”
What are the main objectives for the coming years?
“The experience that we have, allows us to make our own judgement, which is why in this market and with the technological pace of change that we assist daily, we can not think at a high distance, because 6 months can already be considered medium-to-long term. Despite that, and keeping our feet on the ground, our first main goal is to be well succeeded in a market such as the Spanish one, which is five times higher than the Portuguese in terms of people and, perhaps, ten times in terms of turnover.

However, in addition to the new market, we intend to increase our penetration at a national level and also in Mozambique. We believe in our potential and in the possibility of also having, within a horizon of 2 to 3 years, a local presence in other countries. We want to achieve our ideas by reaching for technological innovation, adopting the best practices of the industry and by constantly accompanying the market evolution. All of this is based on the idea of a sustainable growth, transversal to all the company departments.”
What is the first idea (s) that you would like to come into people’s mind when they hear the word "Sendit"?
“My partner in the Communication and Digital Marketing Strategy.”
How do you describe the feeling of representing Sendit?
“It is an honor to be part of Sendit, accompanying the growth and contributing to its success along the years.”
What message would you like to convey to the employees?
“A thank you and confidence message. An appreciation for what we are and confidence in our future. We have a team of multiple valences, experienced, productive, motivated and with an enormous potential to overcome the challenges arising. We are a solid, flexible, dynamic and increasingly agile company, where the difference of opinion is valued and the decisions can be taken quickly, allowing to act with promptness. All of this, allied with a strong technical component, gives us a competitive advantage in the market and the confidence to walk a sustainable growth path to obtain success at all levels.”

On March 14 and 15, another edition of the well-known Retreat of the PDM Group was held at the Vila Galé Évora Hotel.

Diversity Brings Us Together was the theme of this year, which had some news compared to the previous editions. In this event, where the purpose was to encourage the feeling of togetherness among everyone, valuing the diversity present within the Group, the format was different, lasted 2 days and happened elsewhere.

About 210 employees from several companies of the Group were present at the Retreat, namely from PDMFC, Go4Mobility, Go4Communication, Sendit, Scope Invest, Apps4Mobility and MyFarm, who were able to get to know each other, have fun and carry out different activities. The collaborators had the opportunity to realize a Peddy Paper and to attend the Light Talks, a novelty in the Group that had its first edition at the Retreat.


As usual, the event ended with the Group Conference, this time on Saturday afternoon. Luis Landeiro (CEO and CTO of PDMFC), Rui Cordeiro (General Manager of Go4Mobility), Miguel Campos (Chairman and Director of PDMFC’s R&D Department) and Francisco Loureiro (COO and Director of PDMFC’s Solutions Department) shared different topics and perspectives about the future and the strategies for the companies.

It is also worth mentioning the two moments of entertainment, Cante Alentejano and Stand Up, during the dinner and the Saturday Conference, respectively.

Retreat is an annual event that intends to promote relationships between the elements of the teams and companies, improve the internal communication and provide moments of leisure and interaction. You can remember the best moments, anytime you want, in our gallery.

PDMFC
In 2018, PDMFC surpassed its internal goals. The target was to achieve 150 employees, but it already reached, so far, almost 200 employees. Today, it counts with a multidisciplinary team, very focused, diverse and with strong technical skills.

This year, many initiatives were developed with the intent to dynamise the company and to bring the employees closer. Namely, the 25 activities in the scope of the 25 years of the company, the PDM Summer Academy, the implementation of the performance evaluation, as well as the Global Satisfaction survey. Moreover, the company suffered some structural changes, the CEO changed and, due to its growing, the facilities were augmented and improved.

In 2019, it predicts to keep its actual growth and a greater focus on the progress of the employees, investing mostly on trainings and certifications, both in the technical and behavior fields.
PDMFC
MyFarm
MyFarm intends to help the Portuguese farmers to drain their products on a regular basis, through its platform Adelaide.farm. Adelaide.farm is a 100% Portuguese platform, with the main purpose of allowing consumers to receive the best and freshest products at home and the possibility (in the near future) of managing their own Virtual Farm, at the same time that the small producers sell their products in the desired quantities, at fair prices.

The platform offers 4 product categories and 3 terms of sale, and is a connection (currently lost) between the producer and the final costumer. It is also important to mention that about 50% of the products have biological certification. However, all the producers produce in a sustainable and organic way, even without the certification.

Last year, the company saw its income increase. Also in 2018, some events were held before the summer, with the intention of inviting and raising producers to be part of the project. The events took place in Councils and Parish Councils, namely in the Council of Ferreira do Alentejo and Vidigueira, among others. The company was present on the Eco-Schools day of IPBeja, an international program of the “Foundation for Environmental Education”, which aims to encourage actions and recognize the quality work developed by schools in the scope of Environmental Education for Sustainability.
Message to the employees: The work we do daily at MyFarm is much more important than simply leveraging product sales through the Adelaide.farm platform. We create lasting relationships between people who share the same vision - of a sustainable and economically fairer agriculture – in order to make it a reality for those who produce, with better quality for those who consume.
MyFarm

Go4Mobiliity
Go4Mobility defined and planned, for 2018, a set of internal goals that it considers to have attained. The Go4 team has a great collaborative spirit, where the elements talk and accept each other. Nonetheless, it considers that the internal communication can be improved, so that the decisions are communicated in a more linear and assertive way.

The year of 2018 was challenging for Go4Mobility. It introduced itself and opened the Angolan market, trough the creation of a new company, a project that was not previously planned, but that emerged from the necessity of a client and that has demonstrated to have a great potential. The company was also recognized (for the 4th year running) as one of the biggest technology company in Portugal, according to the Exame magazine.

The biggest change this year will be the establishment of the HR Department. The objective is to have someone fully dedicated to the employees, to define trainings, evaluations and dynamics, providing good conditions for the employees so that they fell happy at the workplace.

Message to the employees: Keep on believing in the company and on those that represent it, because the people who did that were all, or almost all, rewarded.

ZPX
2018 was an emotional year for ZPX! The company completed a decade and reached the goals that had set for the year, highlighting the increase in technical training, with investments in new ScrumMaster certifications and in acquisition of literature, documentation and digital technical training.

The participation in worldwide projects, such as “Conan Exiles” and “Mutant Year Zero: Road to Eden”, were instrumental in consolidating the company's portfolio, enabling a strong presence in specialty events, opening doors to new opportunities and leading to another chapter in ZPX’s history.

Recognized for the quality of its works and the substantial investment in the production of an adventure and exploration video game, ZPX announced the acquisition of a majority share of the company by Funcom, in January 2019. Strategically, this acquisition will allow ZPX to grow more rapidly, through the increase of 16 to 50 people on its team by the end of 2020, the increase of turnover and easier access to new projects and markets.

The result of the acquisition, aligned with the privileged geographic location and the culture of knowledge exchange are essential factors to achieve the relevant position that ZPX aspires within the global video game industry. The acquisition also represents a very important milestone in the history of portuguese video game industry, having become a reference at a national level.

The company has also been present in several academic events in Portugal, to elucidate and contribute to a better education of the students who aspire to pursue a career in video game development.
Message to the employees: It is well known that we are going through a phase of change with implications for the company's internal structure and processes. I would like to acknowledge and thank everyone for the dedication and effort that each one has expended to face the challenges we have faced in the last few months and that, nevertheless, continue to cultivate our culture of camaraderie, pro activity and initiative that always characterized us and continues to be one of our most fundamental pillars.
ZPX
